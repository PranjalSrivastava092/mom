BrainWave Problem Statements

Mind Over Matter (MoM) 2k17 brings Brainwave that encourages participants to brain storm and come up with ideas on given topics related to Technology for clean India. They are required to present their innovative ideas through presentations. The participants can present their solutions on any one of the following two problems:



Problem Statement 1


When you refuse to reuse, it’s our India you abuse The problem that our country faces is the lack of recycling infrastructure because of which the waste that can be reused goes vain. Today the solutions to the waste management of our country does not lay solely on waste disposal mechanisms. A major chunk lies in integrating the technology with a system of household-level segregation so that waste does not end up in landfills, but is processed and reused. Come up with your own ideas that can make garbage a useful asset. Show how the power of reusing can bring mind boggling solutions to one of the most pestering problems of the nation.



Problem Statement 2


The recipe for interesting train travels is when cleanliness unravels The problem with our railway system is its hygiene. We lack a robust yet feasible system that can maintain this vast network across the nation. Even though India can boast about having the largest railway network in the world, when it comes to the cleanliness of our railway stations, platforms and washrooms the world looks down upon us. Let’s not forget Indian railways is our responsibility. Keep up with your responsibility and brain storm on how we can maintain the hygiene of our stations, keep our tracks clean and fight with the problems of the sanitation in washrooms and trains. Take your step in making Indian railways not just the biggest but the cleanest through your incredible ideas.



NOTE: Mail your abstracts at mindovermatter2k17@gmail.com with your team name as subject.
