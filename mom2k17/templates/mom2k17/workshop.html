Internet of Things (IoT) Workshop

Internet of Things (IoT), today’s hot topic and tomorrow’s demand, is one of the topics. The internetworking of physical devices, vehicles, buildings and other items—embedded with electronics, software, sensors, actuators, and network connectivity that enable these objects to collect and exchange data is all set to be the next big thing. Gear up to know about its basics, try your hands on arduino, read from sensors, make your first android app, know about cloud servers and much more.



  • Workshop Topics
  • Introduction & Applications of IoT
  • Arduino
  • Programming
  • MIT App Inventor
  • Sensor Interfacing
  • Cloud Computing
  • Projects

Touch and Augmented Reality Workshop

Touch and Augmented reality is a field on which tech giants like Microsoft, Apple and Intel all are working. So would you be behind? Rather than interacting with computer with help of any interface like mouse or keypad interact directly with computer also known as Natural User Interfacing. Change your computing experience and step forward into a completely new era of Motion Sensing & Gesture Computing. Learn the techniques used in surface computing, track your touch and finally make your own multi touch pad.
