About

Mind Over Matter 2k17 - Technology for Clean India

The aim of this Technical Innovation Fair is to put up a show specializing in defying your imagination, breaking boundaries and revolutionizing basic science and technology. The vision is to promote innovation and invention in technology, with focus on technologies aimed at contributing positively to the environment and to the society. It also offers closer interaction between technology seekers and providers. It also encourages creative thinking, providing exploratory experiences to the young minds. Our mission is to encompass as many participants as possible in sharing their knowledge.
