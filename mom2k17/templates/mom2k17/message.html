Message

Faculty Coordinator Mind Over Matter 2k17

It gives me immense pleasure in welcoming you, on behalf of the entire community of the Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management Gwalior to the 3 rd National Technical Innovation Fair, Mind Over Matter (MOM) 2K17 - ‘Technology for Clean India’. Presenting solutions for environmental and social concern for the past two years, this time MOM is dedicated to the much talked about Shri Narendra Modi’s Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. MOM has been an undefeated platform for presenting Innovative ideas by the young minds of our country. We believe that great ideas can and do come from anywhere and so MOM creates an environment conducive not only to the tech savvy graduates but also to the curious and smart school students for their contribution to the society through numerous events awaiting your participation. I invite you all to experience MOM 2K17, the only one of its kind and let yourself free of all the boundaries to explore and innovate.


With best wishes !

Dr. Pankaj Srivastava
Faculty Coordinator
