Flash

The Display Arena | MoM 2k17

Flash is the display arena of MOM 2k17 where an opportunity is given to the innovative minds who created something unique in the sphere of technology. It focuses on bringing out such people and providing them a platform so that they can showcase their work in front of the young minds that will also help them learn and even inspire them to try something on their own.



There is no registration fees for Flash

It is open for all (students, companies, academies, schools, etc.)

Interested partipants are required to send us a mail at mindovermatter2k17@gmail.com describing about what they want to exhibit
